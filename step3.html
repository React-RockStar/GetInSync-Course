Step 3 - Collaborate

Establish the IT organization as the model of collaboration for your enterprise. Build Trust.

Objective

  • Overcome unhealthy IT perceptions
  • Shape demand for IT
  • Identification of business value initiatives

You will

  • Bring marketing thinking to your IT organization and focus on improving your business communications
  • Document inflight programs, projects and link them to strategic business drivers
  • Collaborate with individuals that ‘get it’ and have influence over strategic business programs and project stakeholders
  • Capture high-level current state
